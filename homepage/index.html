My Handknit Sweaters

Last year, I made my first (successful) sweater. Although I've been knitting for 20 years, I'd always thought sweaters were too hard and too complicated. This seemed to be proven right when I made my first sweater in 2021 that was poorly constructed, ill-fitting, and frankly, ugly. I gave up for a couple of years before trying again and made an Orbital sweater which I love and still wear. Since then I've made a few more sweaters and this page is to show them off!

My Sweater Knitting Journey
